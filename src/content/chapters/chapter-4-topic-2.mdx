# Deep Learning in Computer Vision

## Topic 2: Deep Learning in Computer Vision

Deep learning has transformed computer vision by enabling end-to-end learning of image features, leading to unprecedented accuracy in visual recognition tasks.

### Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs)

CNNs are specifically designed for processing grid-like data such as images. They use convolutional layers to automatically detect important features without human intervention.

### Architectures

- LeNet: Early CNN architecture
- AlexNet: Introduced deep learning to computer vision
- VGGNet: Deeper and more accurate
- ResNet: Introduced residual connections
- EfficientNet: Optimized for efficiency and accuracy

### Vision Transformers

Recent advances have shown that transformer architectures can also be applied to image processing, achieving state-of-the-art results in various computer vision tasks.

### Applications

- Medical imaging
- Autonomous vehicles
- Facial recognition
- Quality control in manufacturing

### Challenges

- Data requirements
- Computational complexity
- Interpretability
- Adversarial attacks
